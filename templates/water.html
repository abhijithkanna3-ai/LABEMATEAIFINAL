Formulae

Turbidity Removal Eff. = ((Initial - Final) / Initial) * 100

Detention Time (hr) = (Volume / Flow) * 24

SLR (m³/m²·d) = Flow / Surface Area

FLR (m³/m²·d) = Flow / Filter Area

Chlorine Demand (mg/L) = Dose – Residual

Power (W) = Head × Q × ρ × g

Standard Constants

g (Gravity): 9.81 m/s²

ρ (Water Density): 1000 kg/m³

Recommended DT: 2 - 4 hours

Recommended SLR: 20 - 40 m³/m²·d

Recommended FLR: 120 - 240 m³/m²·d

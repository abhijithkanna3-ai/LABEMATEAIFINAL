Key Formulae

Velocity (V) = 4 × Q / (π × D²)

Reynolds (Re) = (ρ × V × D) / μ

Pressure Drop (ΔP) = P_in - P_out

Hydraulic Power (Pw) = Q × ΔP

Efficiency (η) = (Pw / Pi) × 100

Retention Time (t) = V_separator / Q_oil
